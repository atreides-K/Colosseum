# 🏓 Table Tennis Rules — Spectrum '26

## Tournament Structure

### 1. Knockout Rounds

- Two rounds of knockout to determine the **top 8 teams**
- Match format: **Best of 3 sets**

### 2. League Stage

- 8 qualified teams divided into **2 pools of 4 teams** each
- **Round-robin** format within each pool
- Match format: **Best of 5 sets**

### 3. Semifinals & Finals

- **Top 2 teams from each pool** advance to semifinals
- Semifinal and final: **Best of 5 sets**

## Seeding

Top 8 teams from **Spectrum 2025** receive seeding.

## Match Format

Each match consists of up to **5 games** in this order:

1. Men's Singles
2. Women's Singles
3. Open Singles
4. Open Singles
5. Open Doubles

**First team to win 3 games wins the match.**

## Player Rules

- Each team consists of **5 players**
- A player can participate in at most **1 singles + 1 doubles** match per team match
- Only **1 guest player** allowed per team
- In case of injury, if not all 5 players have been used throughout the tournament, a **replacement** may be found
- **Umpire's decision is final**
