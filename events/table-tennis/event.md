## Format

- 2 knockout rounds → League stage (8 teams in 2 pools of 4, round-robin) → Semifinals → Finals
- Knockout: best of 3 sets
- League / Semis / Finals: best of 5 sets
- Each match: Men's Singles, Women's Singles, 2 Open Singles, Open Doubles
- First to 3 games wins the match
- Seeding based on top 8 teams from Spectrum 2025
