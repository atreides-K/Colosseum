# 🤸 Kho-Kho Rules — Spectrum '26

## Team Composition

- Squad: at least **9**, at most **12** members
- **9 players** on court at a time

## Match Format

- **2 innings**, each of **14 minutes** (7 min chase + 7 min defend)
- **5-minute break** after each chase/defend period
- **Finals:** 9 minutes per inning

## Scoring

- **1 point** per defender out (awarded to chasing team)
- Total score = number of defenders who got out

## Tournament Format

- Depending on number of teams: **Knockout or League** format
- League: **2 points** for a win, **0 for a loss**

## Tiebreak Rules

### Match Tie

- **Sudden death match** for 1 inning
- The time taken for the chasing team to catch the **first opponent runner** determines the winner (lesser time wins)

### League Tie (same points after all matches)

| Situation | Resolution |
|-----------|-----------|
| Top 2 teams tied | Both qualify for finals |
| 2nd & 3rd or 3rd & 4th tied | Sudden death match |
| Top 3 teams tied | Round-robin sudden death |

## General Rules

- **KKFI rules** apply for any aspects not discussed above
- Referee's decision is **final** — arguments will not be entertained
