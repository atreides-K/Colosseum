## Format

- Team match consists of 5 games: Men's Singles, Women's Singles, Men's Doubles, Women's Doubles, Mixed Doubles
- Win by 3 out of 5 games
- League stage: Best-of-3 sets to 21 points
- Knockout format communicated after registration
- Tiebreak: matches won → game difference → point difference → head-to-head

## Rules

- Official shuttle: Yonex Mavis 350
- Non-marking badminton shoes mandatory
- BWF rules apply
- Max 1 male + 1 female guest player; guest locked to one team
- Female guest cannot repeat and plays only 1 game per match
- Team must report on time or face disqualification
