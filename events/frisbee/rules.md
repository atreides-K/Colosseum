# 🥏 Ultimate Frisbee Rules — Spectrum '26

## Team Requirements

- **7 players** on the line with a minimum of **3 female players**
- **3 guest players** allowed on the line
- **Gendered pull** will be followed (details explained to captains after registration)
- All players must have **proper sports shoes** for the turf and follow turf guidelines

## Scoring & Match Duration

- Maximum points: **15**
- **Halftime:** 3-minute break at 8 points or at the halfway mark of total game time (whichever is earlier)
- Each team gets **1 timeout of 75 seconds** per game
- All timeouts including halftime are included in game time
- Match time details released based on number of registrations

## Punctuality

- **Game time starts on schedule** regardless of whether a team is on the line
- Teams are expected to arrive **30 minutes** before the game
- For every **5 minutes late**, the opponent is awarded **1 point**
- **20 minutes late** or forfeit = opponent wins **15–0**
- This rule is strictly followed unless reason is specified beforehand or in case of clashes with other sports

## Game Observer

- A game observer will be on the sideline for all matches
- If a conflict arises and the observer's ruling is requested, it is the **final decision** for both teams

## Contact & Spirit

- Frisbee is a **non-contact sport** — captains are responsible for ensuring safe play by team members
- Minor contacts are acceptable, but player safety must be ensured

## Spirit Circle & MVP

- After each game, both teams traditionally sit together in a **Spirit Circle** to discuss the game
- Teams decide whether to follow this tradition, but captains **must submit the MVP** from the opposite team to the convenors after each game

## Venue

IISc Gymkhana Turf
