## Format

### Match Duration
- **Men's:** 30 min (two 15-min halves, 5-min break); Semis/Finals: 40 min
- **Women's:** 20 min (two 10-min halves); Semis/Finals: 30 min

### Scoring
- 1 point per defender out (chasing team)
- Bonus line: 1 point if 6+ defenders on ground
- League: 3 points for win, 0 for loss

### Tiebreak
- 5 alternate raids by different raiders
- If still tied: Golden Raid after toss
- No substitution during tiebreak/Golden Raid

## Rules

- AKFI rules apply
- Weight restrictions: Men 85kg, Women 75kg max
- Do-or-die raid as per AKFI rules
- Lobby rule (PKL interpretation): raider out of bounds = raid over
- Jewelry/accessories must be removed, nails trimmed
