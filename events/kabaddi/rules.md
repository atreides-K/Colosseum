# 🤼 Kabaddi Rules — Spectrum '26

## Team Composition

- Squad: at most **12 members**, **7 on court** at a time
- Any number of guest players allowed (must be current IISc students)
- A player enrolled for a team **cannot play for other teams**
- Substitution rules as per **AKFI**

## Weight Restrictions

- **Men:** at or below 85 kg
- **Women:** at or below 75 kg

## Match Duration

| Category | Regular | Semis/Finals |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| **Men's** | 30 min (2 × 15 min, 5-min break) | 40 min |
| **Women's** | 20 min (2 × 10 min) | 30 min |

## Scoring

- **1 point** per defender out (awarded to raider/chasing team)
- **Bonus line:** 1 point if raider crosses bonus line with **6+ players** on the ground. No bonus point with 5 or fewer defenders
- **Do-or-die raid** as per AKFI rules

## League Scoring

- **3 points** for a win, **0 points** for a loss
- No draws — tied matches decided by tiebreak procedure

## Tiebreak

1. **5 alternate raids** by different raiders from each team
2. If still tied: **Golden Raid** after a toss
3. No substitution allowed during tiebreak or Golden Raid

## Lobby Rule (PKL Interpretation)

Once the raider goes out of bounds before a tackle, the raid is considered over. Any defender who steps into the lobby in this situation is **not treated as out**.

## General Rules

- All players must **remove jewelry/accessories** and keep **nails trimmed**
- Referee's decision is final
