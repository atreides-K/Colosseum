# ♟️ Chess Rules — Spectrum '26

## Individual Tournament

### Format

- **Swiss System — Blitz (5+3)**
- Number of rounds determined based on total registrations
- Pairings follow the standard Swiss system

### Time Control

| Stage | Time Control |
|-------|-------------|
| All Rounds | 5 min + 3 sec increment (Blitz) |

### Tiebreak Rules

1. **1st place tie:** Armageddon game
2. **Other places:** Standard tiebreak criteria (Buchholz or similar)
3. Final decision on tiebreak method taken by the arbiters

### General Rules

- FIDE rules and etiquette strictly followed
- Decisions of arbiters are final and binding

---

## Team Tournament

### Format

- **Group Stage + Double Elimination**
- Tournament format and time controls may be adjusted based on logistics

### Team Composition

- **4 open players + 1 female player + 2 reserve players**
- Each department may register up to 2 teams
- No changes to team composition permitted once group matches begin

### Match Structure

- Each match played over **5 boards**, with at least 1 female player participating each round
- 2 substitutes per team, operated under a rolling substitution system

### Board Numbering

| Number | Role |
|--------|------|
| 1 | Main team — Board 1 |
| 2 | Main team — Board 2 |
| 3 | Main team — Board 3 |
| 4 | Main team — Board 4 |
| 5 | Main team — Board 5 (Female) |
| 6 | Substitute 1 |
| 7 | Substitute 2 |

- When a substitute is introduced, they play on Board 4; remaining players shift up accordingly
- **Board 5 (Female Board)** can only be substituted by another female player
- If a female player competes on an open board, she may be substituted on the female board by any female player chosen by the captain

### Guest Player Rules

- Maximum **2 guest players** per roster
- Only **1 guest player** may participate in any single match

### Time Controls

| Stage | Time Control |
|-------|-------------|
| Group Stage | 10 min + 5 sec increment |
| Double Elimination | 30 min + 30 sec increment |

### Tiebreak Rules

**Group Stage:**
1. Sum of game points scored across all matches
2. Armageddon: each team's captain nominates one player for a deciding game

**Double Elimination:**
1. 5 vs 5 Blitz (5+3 time control)
2. If still tied, 1 vs 1 Armageddon game

### Player Changes

- Teams may replace **one player** after Group Stage if there is a confirmed scheduling conflict with another sport
- Teams may modify board order after Group Stage
