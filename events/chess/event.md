## Individual Tournament

- **Format:** Swiss System — Blitz (5 min + 3 sec increment)
- FIDE rules apply
- Tiebreak: Armageddon game for 1st place; Buchholz for other places

## Team Tournament

- **Team Size:** 4 open players + 1 female player + 2 reserves (max 2 guests per roster, 1 per match)
- **Format:** Group Stage (10 min + 5 sec) → Double Elimination (30 min + 30 sec)
- 5 boards per match (at least 1 female playing)
- Board 5 (Female Board) can only be substituted by a female player
- Rolling substitution system with 2 substitutes
- Departments may register up to 2 teams
