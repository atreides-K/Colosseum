## Format

- Men's: 5v5 full court
- Women's: 5v5 or 3v3 half court (depending on team sizes)
- Pool-based round robin → Semifinals → Finals
- Pool structure varies by number of teams (2/3/4 pools)

## Rules

- FIBA rules apply
- 4 quarters of 10 minutes each (running time except last minute of final quarter)
- 24-second shot clock (resets to 14 on offensive rebound)
- 5-second violations for throw-in and free throw
- 4 team fouls = 2 free throws; 5 individual fouls = ejection
- Proper sports shoes required, tucked shirts, no jewelry, nails clipped
