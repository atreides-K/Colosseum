# 🎯 Carrom Rules — Spectrum '26

## Team Format

- Team of **5 players** playing 1 singles and 2 doubles
- Each team must have at least one female player
- **Singles:** Player 1
- **Doubles 1:** Player 2 and Player 3
- **Doubles 2:** Player 4 and Player 5
- Team which wins **2 of 3 games** wins the match
- All games will be conducted simultaneously
- The playing order (singles/doubles assignments) must be submitted before every match

## Match Rules

All matches follow the rules at [indiancarrom.co.in](http://www.indiancarrom.co.in/laws-of-carrom/)

### Key Rules

- Each match goes on for **25 points or 45 minutes**, whichever is earlier
- **Queen** = 3 points, other tokens = 1 point each
  - No additional points for queen once you reach 22
- **No communication** during a match (even during doubles) — can be ruled as a foul
- **15 seconds** per shot — failing to shoot in time results in missing your turn
- Players may **not move their chair** while playing (moving within the chair is allowed)
- **Thumb and scissor shots** are allowed
- Playing coins anywhere on the board is allowed
- Break can be made in any way (straight or rebound)

## Penalties / Fouls

| Situation | Penalty |
|-----------|---------|
| Pocket just the striker | 1 token returned, lose turn |
| Pocket striker with a token | 2 tokens returned, turn continues |
| Pocket queen and striker | Queen + 1 of your tokens returned, lose turn |
| Pocket striker while taking cover | 2 tokens returned, turn continues, still get chance to cover queen |
| Pocket your own last coin with striker | Same rule follows (opponent wins if you pocket theirs) |
| Pocket your/opponent's last token with queen still on board | Opponent wins by 3 + number of remaining tokens |
| Coins jumping over the board | Placed at centre, must pay a fine |

> If there is any doubt regarding the rules, it will be cleared before match day.
