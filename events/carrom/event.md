## Format

- Each team plays 3 games: 1 Singles + 2 Doubles
- Only 3 player combinations allowed per team
- Best 2 of 3 games wins the match
- Each game: 25 points or 45 minutes (whichever is earlier)

## Rules

- Indian Carrom rules apply (indiancarrom.co.in)
- Queen = 3 points, other tokens = 1 point
- 15 seconds per shot
- No communication during matches
- No moving chair during play
- Thumb and scissor shots allowed
- Coins jumping over board = fine
