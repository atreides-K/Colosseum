# 💪 Powerlifting Rules — Spectrum '26

## General Rules

- Competition consists of three lifts in order: **Squat, Bench Press, Deadlift**
- Each lifter gets **3 attempts per lift** — best successful attempt counts
- **Total = best Squat + best Bench Press + best Deadlift**
- Tie in total: **lighter lifter ranks higher**

## Rules of Performance

### Squat (Smith Machine)

- Descend until thighs are at least **parallel** (~90° at the knee)
- Controlled descent and ascent required
- No bouncing or rebounding off the bottom
- Knees should not be locked out harshly at the top

### Bench Press

- Head, shoulders, and glutes must stay in contact with the bench
- Feet must remain **flat on the floor**
- Commands: **"Start"** → lower to chest → pause → **"Press"** → lock out → **"Rack"**
- Bar must be lowered to chest, paused motionless, then pressed upward

### Deadlift

- Bar lifted to erect position with **knees locked, shoulders back**
- Hold motionless until referee gives **"Down"** command
- Bar lowered under control with both hands
- Straps allowed, but no hooks that secure to the bar

## Referees & Safety

- Certified or designated trained officials judge the lifts
- Spotters present for safety during all lifts
- A lift is valid if the referee gives a **green signal**
- Referee and organizing committee have **final authority** on all matters
