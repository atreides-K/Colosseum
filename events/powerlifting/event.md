## Weight Categories

**Men:** <60kg, 60–70kg, 70–80kg, 80–90kg, 90+kg

**Women:** <50kg, 50–60kg, 60–70kg, 70–80kg, 80+kg

## Format

- 3 lifts: Squat, Bench Press, Deadlift
- 3 attempts per lift, best counts
- Total = Squat + Bench + Deadlift
- Tiebreak: lighter lifter ranks higher

### Scoring (by placement)
- 1st: 100 pts | 2nd: 80 pts | 3rd: 60 pts | 4th: 40 pts

## Rules

- Smith Machine for squats
- Bench: head/shoulders/glutes on bench, feet on floor
- Deadlift: erect position, knees locked, shoulders back
- Straps allowed for deadlifts only

### Equipment Allowed
- Leather belts (10cm wide, 13mm thick)
- Wrist/knee wraps or knee sleeves (not both)
- Deadlift straps (bring your own)
- Chalk provided
