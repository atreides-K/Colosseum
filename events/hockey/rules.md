# 🏑 Hockey Rules — Spectrum '26

## Team Formation

- Teams formed from **individual registrations** and may be **mixed across departments**
- Each team: **12–16 players**, with **11 on field** including a goalkeeper

## Match Format

- Played on **full ground** following standard format
- **4 quarters of 15 minutes** each, with short breaks
- **FIH rules** followed for gameplay

## Substitutions

- **Rolling substitutions** (on/off allowed during play)

## Match Day

- Teams must report **10–15 minutes** before match time
- Umpire/organizer decisions are **final**

## Tiebreak

- In case of a draw: **shootout**
- If still tied: **sudden death**

## Safety

- Basic safety gear is recommended
- Unsafe play may lead to **removal from the match**
- Organizers may make minor adjustments based on logistics
