# 🎮 Foosball Rules — Spectrum '26

## Tournament Format

- **28 teams** divided into **4 pools** of 7 teams each
- Pooling by partial seeding — no two teams from the same department in the same pool

### Pool Stage
- **Knockout format** within each pool
- Each pool produces **1 pool topper**

### Round Robin Stage
- The 4 pool toppers advance to a round robin stage
- Each team plays against every other team
- Standings determined by: **Most sets won** → Goal difference (tiebreak)

### Finals
- **Top 2 teams** → Final
- **3rd & 4th teams** → Bronze Medal Match

### Individual Awards
- **MVP (Male)** and **MVP (Female)** trophies awarded
- Based on highest number of goals scored (round robin stage only)

## Match Structure

Matches are played between departments. Each team plays **3 games** in the following order:

1. Open Doubles
2. Mixed Doubles
3. Open Doubles

## Team Composition

- Only **one player** may participate in a maximum of **2 matches**
- All other players may play only **1 match** each
- Teams may have **1 additional backup player** (apart from the main 5). The backup may only participate if they have not played for any other team

## Gameplay Rules

- Each game is played to **10 points** (5 points from each side of the table)
- Teams **switch sides** after one team reaches 5 points
- **Coin toss** determines the initial serve
- After the serve, the ball must touch at least **2 foosball figures** before a goal can be scored
- A **self-goal** scored in a single touch is valid
- If the ball is **trapped**, it must be released or passed within **15 seconds**
- If the ball becomes **dead at the defenders**, play restarts from defender position once the opposing team is ready
- If the ball goes **out of the table** or becomes unreachable, it will be re-served by the team that last served
- Each team is allowed **1 timeout per match**

## Fair Play and Fouls

The referee's decision is **final** in all situations.

**Not allowed:**
- Distracting the opponent in any form
- Shaking, lifting, or tilting the table
- Spinning the rods beyond 360 degrees

**Penalties for violations:**
1. Warning
2. Loss of point
3. Forfeit of the match
