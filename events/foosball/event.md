## Format

- Tournament format TBA after registrations close
- Each match: 3 games (2 Open Doubles + 1 Mixed Doubles)
- Game to 10 points (switch sides at 5)
- Only 1 player can play in max 2 games; all others play 1 game each

## Rules

- Ball must touch 2+ foosball figures before a goal counts
- Trapped ball: 15 seconds to release or pass
- No spinning rods more than 360 degrees
- No table shaking
- Self-goals are valid
- 1 timeout per match per team
- Penalties: warning → loss of point → forfeit
