# 🚴 Cycling Rules — Spectrum '26

## Categories

1. Men Road Bike
2. Women Road Bike
3. Men Hybrid / Mountain Bike
4. Women Hybrid / Mountain Bike

> Road bikes and non-road bikes are distinguished based on **drop handlebars only**. If fewer than 5 road bike participants register per category, all participants race in a single category.

## Event Structure

### Qualifiers (Individual Time Trial)

- Held only if **>20 registrations** per category
- Starting times provided at least a day before
- Race timer starts at mentioned time regardless of participant starting
- **2 laps** of the route
- After 2 laps, head straight to finish at Main Building entrance on Gulmohar Marg
- **Draft-illegal:** minimum **5m separation** between riders (ideally 50m). Overtake quickly or fall back
- Ride must be recorded on **Strava/Wahoo/Komoot/Garmin** to verify no shortcuts
- Re-trial may be allowed if affected by traffic obstruction (at organizers' discretion)

### Finals (Road Race)

| Category | Laps |
|----------|------|
| Men Road Bike | 7 + 1 |
| Women Road Bike | 4 + 1 |
| Men Hybrid / Mountain Bike | 5 + 1 |
| Women Hybrid / Mountain Bike | 2 + 1 |

- **Rolling start** behind a motorized vehicle at 10–15 km/h, pulling away at E-block entrance
- Rider on pole (fastest qualifier) dictates pace after E-block — no one may overtake the pole
- Shuffling among non-pole finalists is permitted
- **Overtakes permitted** after the right turn towards Digits
- Last lap warning announced when starting final lap
- Winner is the rider who crosses the finish line first on Gulmohar Marg
- After the winner crosses, the right turn towards Digits is closed; lapped riders finish at end of current lap
- Race must be GPS-recorded (at minimum 1st, 2nd, 3rd positions)

## Route

![Cycling Route Map](rules/cycling-route.png)

- Starts at Library entrance on Gulmohar Marg
- Loop includes fast straights, tricky turns, and short climbs
- Route marshals at checkpoints to prevent route-cutting

## Safety & Equipment

- **Helmets mandatory** — no helmet, no ride. Ensure proper fit
- **Working brakes** required
- **No e-bikes** (no battery/fuel powered motors)
- Participants should bring water/glucose and essentials

## General Instructions

- Racing numbers distributed before each slot
- All participants must report at reporting time regardless of individual start time
- Safety instructions and any route changes announced at reporting
