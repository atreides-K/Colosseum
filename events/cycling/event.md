## Format

- **Qualifiers:** Individual Time Trial (2 laps) — only if >20 registrations per category
- **Finals:** Road racing with laps
  - Men Road: 7+1 laps
  - Women Road: 4+1 laps
  - Men Hybrid: 5+1 laps
  - Women Hybrid: 2+1 laps
- Rolling start with motorized vehicle pacing on first lap
- Rider on pole (fastest qualifier) dictates pace

## Rules

- Helmets mandatory
- Working brakes required
- No e-bikes allowed
- GPS tracking required (Strava/Wahoo/Komoot/Garmin)
- Qualifier is draft-illegal (5m minimum separation)
- No shortcuts — course marshals monitor the route
- Overtakes permitted after turn towards Digits
