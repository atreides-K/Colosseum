# 🏐 Throwball Rules — Spectrum '26

## Team Composition

- **12 players** per team: 7 active + 5 substitutes
- Maximum **2 guest players** per team — teams with more than 2 guests will be disqualified

## Court & Net

- Court: **12.2m × 18.3m** with 1m neutral box on either side
- Net height: **2.2m**

## Match Format

- **Best of 3 sets**, 25 points per set (rally scoring)
- Toss winner chooses service or court
- Team that served in 1st set receives in 2nd set

## Player Positions

- Players must stand in a **2-3-2 position** at the time of service
- At least **3 players** must serve per team, alternating each time after a side-over

## Service Rules

- Ball must be released from **above shoulder level**
- Served using the **palm** — ball should not touch any other body part
- Must serve **within 5 seconds** after the whistle
- Service ball must **not touch the net**
- Server must not enter the court before the ball crosses the net
- Serve from the **service zone** without crossing the end line

## Rally Rules

- Players must **catch with both hands** and **return with one hand** only
- Ball must be released **within 3 seconds** of being caught
- **Double touches** not allowed (service or rally)
- Ball falling on the **box line or dead zone** = foul
- If ball slips or rebounds from one player's hands, another player **cannot catch and return** it (foul)
- No body part should touch the box line while receiving or returning
- Players may **shift the ball** from right to left or vice versa
- A player can **jump when throwing** (ball may touch the net but not the antenna); must have ground contact when catching
- **Simultaneous catching** by 2+ players = foul (clashing)

## Timeouts

- **2 timeouts of 30 seconds** per set

## Disputes

- Only the team **captain** may speak to the referee
- Referee's decision is **final** — arguments lead to team disqualification
- In case of crowd/disturbance interference, referee may call a **re-serve**
